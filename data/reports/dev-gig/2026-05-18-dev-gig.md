## Quick-Start Recommendation

### Pick 1: AI Wrapper SaaS — Fastest ROI
Choose one vertical you already understand (legal, real estate, fitness, HR, e-commerce) and wrap Claude or GPT around a single painful repetitive workflow. Example: "auto-generate client intake summaries for solo law practices" — charge $49–$99/month. Use Lovable or Cursor to build in 2–3 weeks with minimal backend. Distribute via Indie Hackers for early feedback, then submit to AppSumo for volume. Solo founders are reaching $1K–$10K MRR within 3–6 months on this model. **First action:** spend 2 hours today interviewing 5 people in your target industry to find their single most repetitive text or data task.

### Pick 2: AI-Powered Browser Extension — Lowest Build Friction
Extensions require only HTML/CSS/JavaScript and a Chrome Manifest V3 scaffold — no backend needed for simple tools, which means a solo dev can ship in 2–3 weeks. Target an existing tool people already use daily (LinkedIn, Gmail, Notion, GitHub) and add one AI feature: summarize, rewrite, extract, or auto-fill. Monetize via a $4–$9/month subscription using ExtensionPay or a one-time $19 purchase. Successful extensions report $10K–$130K/month at scale, with Chrome's 3.45B users providing distribution. **First action:** install the top 3 Chrome extensions in your target category, read their 1-star reviews, and identify the single most-requested missing feature as your product.

### Pick 3: API Integration Micro-tools — Steadiest Freelance Income
Every mid-size company has at least one broken or missing integration between their CRM, invoicing tool, project manager, and communication stack. Build productized connectors as fixed-scope, fixed-price packages (e.g., "Notion → HubSpot contact sync for $500"). List on Upwork with a clear Project Catalog entry. After delivering 3–5 similar projects, package the reusable connector as a self-service SaaS on LemonSqueezy at $19–$49/month. API integration commands $50–$100/hr on Upwork and $100–$150/hr on Toptal with 178% YoY spending growth in AI integrations. **First action:** post an Upwork profile today with one specific integration you can deliver in under a week; price it at $300–$500 fixed to win your first client fast.
